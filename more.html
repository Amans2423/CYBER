🚦 Traffic Light System for Online Safety

Green ✅: Safe sites like school websites

Yellow ⚠️: Be cautious - social media, unknown emails

Red ❌: NEVER share details or click unknown links

🕵️‍♂️ How to Spot Fake Websites?

  • Look for 🔒 HTTPS (not just HTTP)
  • Check for weird spellings in links
  • Avoid sites with too many pop-ups

🤖 AI & Cyber Security – How AI Helps?

  • AI helps detect hackers faster!
  • It scans emails and stops spam.
  • AI chatbots can also be dangerous (don't share info!).
